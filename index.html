How Melba works
Everything you need to know before you begin
🔒 Your privacy
👤
Your answers are just for you. Your partner never sees your exact words — only the joint recommendation that comes from combining both your preferences.
🔐
Sessions expire after 1 hour. Nothing is stored beyond that.
💌 How it works
1️⃣
You answer 5 short questions about your mood, what you're curious about, and how adventurous you're feeling tonight.
2️⃣
You get a magic link to share with your partner. They answer their own 5 questions privately.
3️⃣
Melba combines both sets of answers and recommends the experience that fits you both — unlocking automatically when your partner finishes.
✨ Meet your Intimacy Coach
After your recommendation, you'll have access to Melba's AI coach — a warm, knowledgeable guide trained on sexual wellbeing research. Ask anything: what to try next, how to bring something up with your partner, or just explore what you're curious about.
